WORKSHOPS

Kubernetes 101

Come join us and explore how to manage applications at scale using Kubernetes. In this workshop, we'll take a sample app, build a container for it, then deploy and scale it using Kubernetes. It'll also cover aspects of a production grade service like autoscaling, monitoring, logging and troubleshooting.

Nathen Harvey, VP of Community Development at Chef, helps the community whip up an awesome ecosystem built around the Chef platform. Nathen also spends much of his time helping people learn about the practices, processes, and technologies that support DevOps, continuous delivery, and high velocity organizations.
