About DevOpsDays India

DevOpsDays India is a global event complementing other DevOpsDays events across the world. You can find more about DevOpsDays global event here

This year our event is 2 days single track event especially focussed on containers based theme. While we welcome all kinds of talks, we would love to have more container talks. We are lining up speakers from well known international community and companies such as PagerDuty, Chef etc. Please don't forget to submit your RFP here
